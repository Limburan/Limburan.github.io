On Fridays there is a market in the village. We planned to all go today (Christine, our boat pilot and his wife, and me), but the boat pilot wanted to see his kids last night, so took his wife to the village and said they'll do the shopping for us. Given that they were also carrying a bag full of elephant dung, we weren't protesting too much about not being included in the motorcycle trip.

Our night survey was busy: we were radiotracking Christine's crocodiles, doing a population survey, and also trying out my playback experiment for the first time. The water level is even lower, so again we had to get out and push the boat a lot, and still got stuck several times!
