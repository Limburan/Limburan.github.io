## Education and research experience

*Thesis title*: Acoustic communication of rare and threatened crocodilians and its use for population monitoring. <br>
*Description*: Established species-specific vocal repertoires and tested acousticmonitoring methods for crocodile populations in Southeast Asia and West Africa.

*Supervisor*: Prof Gareth Jones (University of Bristol) <br>
*Thesis title*: Ecology and conservation of juvenile crocodilians *Tomistoma schlegelii* and *Crocodylus siamensis* in Mesangat Lake, East Kalimantan, Indonesia. <br>
*Description*: Investigated ecological niche partitioning between sympatric crocodilians in a threatened wetland.
